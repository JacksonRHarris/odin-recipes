Delicious Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • tomato sauce
  • pasta
  • ground beef
  • mozarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • basil

Directions: In a large pyrex baking tray, layer tomato sauc, pasta, cheese, and meat approximately five or six times or until the top of the baking tray is reached

Steps: Bake at 400 degrees Farenheit for an hour and ten minutes.
